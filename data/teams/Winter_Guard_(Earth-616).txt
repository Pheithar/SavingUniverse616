| Overview                = The '''Winter Guard''' (''Russian:'' Зимняя гвардия)<!-- {{r|Official Handbook of the Marvel Universe A-Z Vol 1 13}} --> are a government sponsored team of extraordinary individuals with special abilities. They fight for the people of the [[Russian Federation]] and their interests around the globe. The team is formed from members of the [[Soviet Super-Soldiers (Earth-616)|Soviet Super-Soldiers]] the existed before the fall of the [[Soviet Union]] and the end of the [[Cold War (Event)|Cold War]].{{r|Official Handbook of the Marvel Universe A-Z Vol 1 13}}{{r|Official Handbook of the Marvel Universe: Teams 2005 Vol 1 1}} They have  been described as "Russia's answer for the [[Avengers (Earth-616)|Avengers]]."{{r|She-Hulk Vol 2 34}}

| HistoryText             = 

===Origin===
After the fall of the [[USSR]] the newly formed Russia decided they needed their own heroic team to rival the Americans. They merged the [[People's Protectorate (Earth-616)|People's Protectorate]] with [[Siberforce (Earth-616)|Siberforce]], becoming the Winter Guard.{{r|Hulk: Winter Guard Vol 1 1}} [[Josef Petkus (Earth-616)|Josef Petkus]], the "Red Guardian," became the leader, now known as "Steel Guardian" along with [[Laynia Petrovna (Earth-616)|Darkstar]], [[Nikolai Krylenko (Earth-616)|Vanguard]], [[Illich Lavrov (Earth-616)|Sibercat]], [[Vostok (Anatoly) (Earth-616)|Vostok]], [[Fantasia (Earth-616)|Fantasia]] and [[Illarion Pavlovich Ramskov (Earth-616)|Powersurge]].{{r|Iron Man Vol 3 9}}

===Battling the Mandarin===
The Winter Guard encountered the American hero [[Anthony Stark (Earth-616)|Iron Man]] on his hunt to find the [[Mandarin (Earth-616)|Mandarin]]. He encountered them mid battle as they were taking on a group of mercenaries. He explained that the Mandarin was responsible for the attacked and asked the them to aid him, but they refused to abandon Russia.{{r|Iron Man Vol 3 9}} Later the [[Avengers (Earth-616)|Avengers]] came Russia to aid them in battling [[Dragon of Heaven]]. They battled the Mandarin's forces and attempted to enter the Dragon of Heaven, [[Vostok (Anatoly) (Earth-616)|Vostok]] tried to use his powers to control the vessel, only be taken over by the dragon instead. But Iron Man was able to destroy the Dragon of Heaven.{{r|Iron Man Vol 3 10}}

===Sandstorm===
The Winter Guard, using [[Laynia Petrovna (Earth-616)|Darkstar]]'s powers, attempted to save the people in the town of [[Irkutsk]] when a mysterious sandstorm hit the town.{{r|Thunderbolts Vol 1 25}}

[[File:Winter Guard (Earth-616) from Hulk Vol 2 1 0001.jpg|250px|thumb|right|Winter Guard]]
===Kree Invasion===
The guard battle a group of mysterious aliens in the [[Smolensk]].{{r|Maximum Security Vol 1 1}} It was revealed the invasions was caused by the [[Kree]] who had now become the [[Ruul]]. They teamed up with the Avengers and the other heroes of Earth to fight back.{{r|Maximum Security Vol 1 3}}

===The Presence===
The [[Sergei Krylov (Earth-616)|Presence]] returned and planned to re-unite the Soviet Union by transforming its people into a race of zombie-like radioactive beings living under a communal mind. When the Winter Guard and the Avengers arrived he took control of their minds as well. His allies; [[Tania Belinskaya (Earth-616)|Starlight]], ultimately offered a surrender and used her own power to revive those who had been transformed and remanded herself and the Presence to Russian custody.{{r|Avengers Vol 3 42|44}}

===U.L.T.I.M.A.T.U.M.===
The team faced [[Underground Liberated Totally Integrated Mobile Army To Unite Mankind (Earth-616)|U.L.T.I.M.A.T.U.M.]] lead by [[Karl Morgenthau (Earth-616)|Flag-Smasher]] in [[Novosibirsk]], who hoped to destroy sovereignty of nations throughout the world.{{r|Citizen V and the V-Battalion: The Everlasting Vol 1 3}}

===Death of Abomination===
[[Boris Vadim (Earth-616)|Crimson Dynamo]] teamed up with [[Mikhail Ursus (Earth-616)|Ursa Major]], a new Darkstar [[Sasha Roerich (Earth-616)|Sasha Roerich]], and a mysterious new [[Red Guardian (Anton) (Earth-616)|Red Guardian]]. The new Winter Guard confronted [[Leonard Samson (Earth-616)|Doc Samson]] who was in Russia tracking a [[Thaddeus Ross (Earth-616)|Hulk-like creature]] who bested the [[Emil Blonsky (Earth-616)|Abomination]] and shot and killed him with a giant gun. [[Iron Man]], [[She-Hulk]], [[Thaddeus Ross (LMD) (Earth-616)|General Ross]] and [[Maria Hill (Earth-616)|Maria Hill]] from [[Strategic Homeland Intervention, Enforcement and Logistics Division (Earth-616)|S.H.I.E.L.D.]] were also there examining Blonsky's corpse. They fought the Americans until Ross found a survivor of the battle a little boy who kept murmuring the word "Красногоin" which in English means "Red."{{r|Hulk Vol 2 1}}

===[[Secret Invasion]]===
During the [[Secret Invasion|Skrull invasion]], a new version of the Winter Guard appeared, similar to the Avengers/50-State Initiative. [[James Rhodes (Earth-616)|War Machine]] descended back toward Earth after destroying a portion of the Skrull fleet. In an attempt to assist Russia in preventing the Skrulls from detonating all of the country's nuclear weaponry, War Machine engaged, but the Winter Guard were ordered to "prioritize" their targets. After War Machine escaped capture, the Winter Guard assisted him in taking down the last few ships of the Skrull fleet that were attacking Russia.{{r|Iron Man: Director of S.H.I.E.L.D. Vol 1 34|35}}

===Invasion of Marinmer===
The Guard were sent to [[Marinmer]] after an earthquake to protect the interests of Darqon Par. They battled [[Lady Liberators (Earth-616)|Lady Liberators]] who also arrived to help. The two battled but during the fight [[She-Hulk]] was able to convince  Red Guardian that the ruler of the country was dictator and he orders the Winter Guard to stand down and help the people.{{r|She-Hulk Vol 2 34|36}}

===Death of Darkstar===
Darkstar was killed sacrificing herself to destroy [[Igor Drenkov (Earth-616)|Igor Drenkov]]; however, Vadim died in battle and was replaced by [[Galina Nemirovsky (Earth-616)|Galina Nemirovsky]] as the new Crimson Dynamo.<!-- {{r|Hulk: Winter Guard Vol 1 1}} --> Darkstar was replaced by [[Reena Stancioff (Earth-616)|Reena Stancioff]] who was given her DNA but also had her latent memories. She was with the team when they faced the [[Remont 4 (Earth-616)|Remont 6]].{{r|Hulk: Winter Guard Vol 1 1}} The team faced the invading [[Atlanteans]] led by [[Krang (Earth-616)|Krang]]{{r|Darkstar and the Winter Guard Vol 1 1}} But the new Darkstar  died fighting [[Fantasia (Earth-616)|Fantasma]] and the [[Dire Wraiths]]. One of Wraiths took the form of Darkstar and the possession of the gem, giving it her powers.{{r|Darkstar and the Winter Guard Vol 1 2}} It attempted to use the [[:Glossary:Darkforce|Darkforce]] powers on the team and the [[Protectorate (Earth-616)|Protectorate]], the Wraith was overwhelmed by her personality, resulting in a seemingly reborn Laynia. The teams defeated Fantasma and the [[Sergei Krylov (Earth-616)|Presence]] destroying the base.{{r|Darkstar and the Winter Guard Vol 1 3}}

===King Hyperion===
[[Mark Milton (Earth-4023)|Hyperion]] came to the nation of [[Uzbekistan]] where he began to rampage until confronted by the Winter Guard. Defeating the team, he then found himself face-to-face with [[Adam Brashear (Earth-616)|Blue Marvel]] who defeated Hyperion.{{r|Age of Heroes Vol 1 3}}

===Intelligencia===
They attempted to capture the super-villain group [[Intelligencia (Earth-616)|Intelligencia]], who used the Zero Cannon on them and sent the heroes to space.{{r|Amazing Spider-Man Vol 1 676}} They later proved to have survived the experience.<ref>[http://forums.comicbookresources.com/showthread.php?396699-The-Former-Mutant-Formerly-Known-as-Darkstar-s-new-status-quo-(ASM-spoilers)&p=14411791#post14411791]</ref>

===Defenders of the Deep===
In response to the increase of global tensions, marked by the Avengers answering to [[T'Challa (Earth-616)|King T'Challa]] of [[Wakanda]] and the formation of the [[Defenders of the Deep (Earth-616)|Defenders of the Deep]] by [[Namor McKenzie (Earth-616)|Namor]] to drive off human presence in the oceans, the government of Russia revamped the Winter Guard. In addition of Ursa Major being brought back into the team from imprisonment and Dimitri Bukharin returning from retirement to lead the team, the Winter Guard saw the addition of [[Vostok (Anatoly) (Earth-616)|Vostok]], the [[Dievas|Slavic gods]] [[Perun (Earth-616)|Perun]] and [[Chernobog (Earth-616)|Chernobog]], and the mysterious [[Red Widow (Winter Guard) (Earth-616)|Red Widow]], the latter being tasked with observing the team and reporting to their superiors.

The Winter Guard intercepted the Defenders of the Deep during an attack on [[Hydropolis]] in retaliation to Namor's attack against Russian submarines in the [[Black Sea]]. The Avengers were already in the scene, and initially worked in tandem with the Winter Guard. In-fighting between Winter Guard members Perun and Chernobog resulted in [[Jennifer Walters (Earth-616)|She-Hulk]] attacking them, breaking out a fight between the Avengers and the Russian heroes. The battle came to a standstill after the Defenders escaped, and the Red Widow forced Crimson Dynamo to have his team retreat.{{r|Avengers Vol 8 10}}
