| History                 = Avius is a member of the [[Inhumans (Inhomo supremis)|Inhumans]], an offshoot of humanity that gains super-human abilities via exposure to the [[Terrigen Mist]]. He was gifted with a bird like form thanks to exposure to the Terrigen Mist. His earliest recorded appearance was when he, [[Flaidermaus (Earth-616)|Flaidermaus]] and [[Pinyon (Earth-616)|Pinyon]] were sent out to incapacitate the [[Fantastic Four (Earth-616)|Fantastic Four's]] [[Jonathan Storm (Earth-616)|Human Torch]] for their leader [[Blackagar Boltagon (Earth-616)|Black Bolt]]. {{r|Fantastic Four #129}} Later, Avius was attacked and severely injured by [[Blastaar (Earth-616)|Blastaar]]. He was found by [[Leonus (Earth-616)|Leonus]] and returned to [[Attilan]] just in time to warn [[Blackagar Boltagon (Earth-616)|Black Bolt]] of the approaching danger{{r|Inhumans #1}}. When [[Maximus Boltagon (Earth-616)|Maximus]] was freed by the Kree and took over Atillan, Avius managed to escape along with a few others.{{r|Inhumans Vol 1 #5}}
