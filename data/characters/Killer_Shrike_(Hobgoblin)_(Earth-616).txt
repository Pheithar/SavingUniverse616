| History                 = ===Becoming Killer Shrike===
After [[Simon Maddicks (Earth-616)|Simon Maddicks]] sold the original Killer Shrike suit to the [[Roderick Kingsley (Earth-616)|Hobgoblin]], he sold the suit to an unnamed criminal. 

===Goblin War===
Killer Shrike was present during a battle between Hobgoblin's forces and the [[Goblin Underground (Earth-616)|Goblin Underground]]. He, along with every other Hobgoblin franchise, joined Team Goblin when [[Norman Osborn (Earth-616)|Green Goblin]] apparently killed Hobgoblin, really Kingsley's butler [[Hobgoblin (Claude) (Earth-616)|Claude]].{{r|Superior Spider-Man Vol 1 26}}

Killer Shrike was among the former Hobgoblin minions that encountered [[Maxwell Dillon (Earth-616)|Electro]] at the [[Bar with No Name]].{{r|Amazing Spider-Man Vol 3 1}}

Killer Shrike was among the villains seen at the funeral for [[Francine Frye (Earth-616)|Francine Frye]] that was held at the Bar with No Name at the time when [[Felicia Hardy (Earth-616)|Black Cat]] arrived looking for assistance against [[Peter Parker (Earth-616)|Spider-Man]]. He was later among the villains that sided with Black Cat when she was forming her [[Black Cat's Gang (Earth-616)|gang]].{{r|Amazing Spider-Man Vol 3 6}}

Killer Shrike was among the villains rounded by [[John Myers (Earth-616)|Missile Mate]] and brought to [[Phillip Urich (Earth-616)|Goblin King]] where Missile Mate claimed that Hobgoblin "abandoned" them.{{r|AXIS: Hobgoblin Vol 1 2}}

Black Cat later had Killer Shrike and [[Melter (Hobgoblin) (Earth-616)|Melter]] beat up [[Ringer (Hobgoblin) (Earth-616)|Ringer]].{{r|Amazing Spider-Man Vol 3 16}}

Killer Shrike later joined [[Fritz von Meyer (Earth-616)|Swarm]]'s [[Sinister Six (Earth-616)|Sinister Six]] when they attacked Spider-Man and the students of the [[Xavier's School for Gifted Youngsters|Jean Grey School for Higher Learning]]. After Swarm was defeated, the villains surrendered.{{r|Spider-Man and the X-Men Vol 1 4}}
