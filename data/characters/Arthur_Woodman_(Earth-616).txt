Mr. Woodman was an agent of [[Hydra (Earth-616)|Hydra]] a well as a [[United States Senate (Earth-616)|Senator]], who worked to corrupt [[Roger Brokeridge (Earth-616)|Roger Brokeridge]], also known as [[Hardball]], to insure that Hydra had someone on the inside of [[Camp Hammond]] and used him to steal a vial of [[Super-Power Inhibiting Nanobots]].{{r|Avengers: The Initiative Vol 1 3}}

He hired the [[Vulturions (Earth-616)|Vulturions]] to steal [[Wernher von Blitzschlag (Earth-616)|Baron von Blitzschlag]]'s classified government documents concerning the greatest gamma ray experiments.{{r|Avengers: The Initiative #7}}

Woodman was informed by [[Henry Gyrich (Earth-616)|Secretary Gyrich]] about the experiments concerning the [[Tactigon]] and the [[Gauntlet Armor]].{{r|Avengers: The Initiative Annual #1}}

After the [[Secret Invasion]], Hardball's status as an agent for Hydra led to a confrontation against the rest of his [[Heavy Hitters (Earth-616)|Heavy Hitters]] team and a visiting [[Melati Kusuma (Earth-616)|Komodo]]. Hardball killed Senator Woodman after the man used Komodo's [[Connors Formula]] to turn himself into a monster, Hardball then effectively became the new leader of Woodman's Hydra cell.{{r|Avengers: The Initiative Special #1}}
