===Overview===
The '''United States Army''' is the land warfare service branch of the [[United States Armed Forces (Earth-616)|United States Armed Forces]]. It is designated as the Army of the [[United States of America]] in the U.S. Constitution.

===Origin===
[[File:Benjamin_Franklin_(Earth-616)_from_S.H.I.E.L.D._Vol_1_4_001.jpg|left|thumb]]
The modern U.S. Army has its roots in the Continental Army which was formed to fight in the [[American Revolutionary War]] against the [[British Army (Earth-616)|British Army]]. After the war, the newly formed nation created the United States Army on 3 June 1784.

===[[War of 1812]]===
The War of 1812, the second and last war between the USA and [[United Kingdom|Great Britain]], had mixed results. 

===[[Mexican–American War]]===
The war with the Mexican Army lasted from 1846 to 1848. The war lead to the acquisition of the territory that would later become the states [[California (State)|California]], [[Nevada (State)|Nevada]], [[Utah (State)|Utah]], [[Colorado (State)|Colorado]], [[Arizona (State)|Arizona]], [[Wyoming (State)|Wyoming]], and [[New Mexico (State)|New Mexico]].

===[[American Civil War (Event)|American Civil War]]===
In 1860 eleven pro-slavery southern states seceded from the Union and formed the [[Confederate States of America]], triggering a civil war with the northern states. In 1865 the [[Confederate States Army (Earth-616)|Confederate Army]] was defeated uniting the union once again.{{r|Fin Fang Four Return! Vol 1 1}}

===[[Spanish-American War]]===
Although most of the key battles of the war against the [[Spanish Empire]] was won by the [[United States Navy (Earth-616)|U.S. Navy]]. The army defeated [[Spain]] in land campaigns in [[Cuba]]. After the U.S. victory, Cuba, [[Guam]], [[Puerto Rico]], and the [[Philippines]] came under control of the United States. Following that war, the American created the military base [[Guantanamo Bay]].{{r|Marvel Atlas Vol 1 2}}

===[[Boxer Rebellion]]===
In 1899 A.D., U.S. troops intervened in the [[Boxer Rebellion]] in [[China]].{{r|Semper Fi Vol 1 4}}

===[[World War I]]===
In 1917 A.D. the United States joined [[World War I]].{{r|Marvel Atlas Vol 1 2}}

===[[World War II]]===
[[File:World War II from Captain America Vol 1 601 001.jpg|230px|right|thumb|Soldiers during WWII]]
In 1941 A.D. Japanese warplanes attacked the U.S. fleet at [[Pearl Harbor]], [[Oahu]], [[Hawaii (State)|Hawaii]]. The U.S. declared war on Japan; [[Germany]] declared war on U.S., which thereafter intervened on a massive scale in World War II.

At the end of 1941 the U.S. Military began [[Project Rebirth (Earth-616)|Project: Rebirth]] which created the [[Super-Soldier Serum]] that turned [[Steven Rogers (Earth-616)|Steve Rogers]] into Captain America. Throughout the war, Cap and his partner [[James Buchanan Barnes (Earth-616)|Bucky Barnes]]{{r|Captain America Comics Vol 1 1}} fought alongside infantry and with a group of heroes known as the [[Invaders (Earth-616)|Invaders]].{{r|Avengers Vol 1 71}} In 1943, the [[United States Government (Earth-616)|U.S. Government]] forcibly recruited 300 African-American soldiers to serve as test subjects to recreate the [[Super Soldier Serum]]; however, only one man survived the gruesome process: [[Isaiah Bradley (Earth-616)|Isaiah Bradley]].{{r|Truth: Red, White & Black Vol 1 3|4}}

[[File:Normandy from 4 Vol 1 4 001.png|250px|left|thumb|Invasion of [[Normandy]]]]
In 1945, the closing months of World War II, Captain America and Bucky were both presumed dead in an explosion.{{r|What If? Vol 1 4}} Later in 1945, the U.S. dropped two atomic bombs on [[Hiroshima]] and [[Nagasaki]] which led to Japan's surrender.

===[[Cold War (Event)|Cold War]]===
After World War II, the United States and the [[Soviet Union]] directly threatened each other with nuclear weapons. This period of time is referred to as The Cold War. The countries never actually fought but did fund and support wars over a 40 year period.

====[[Korean War]]====
In 1950, the Korean War began when [[North Korea]] invaded [[South Korea]] following a series of clashes along the border. The United States as the principal force came to the aid of South.{{r|Combat Kelly Vol 1 1}}

====[[Vietnam War]]==== 
The Vietnam War lasted from 1954 to 1975. It involved the deployment of U.S. troops to [[Vietnam]] to fight the communist insurgence. In 1973 [[North Vietnam]] and [[South Vietnam]] continued fighting but without significant U.S. involvement. It ended with communist victory in 1975 with the fall of [[Ho Chi Minh City|Saigon]] (later renamed Ho Chi Minh City).{{r|Captain America: Theatre of War - Ghosts of My Country Vol 1 1}}

===[[Gulf War]]===
In 1991, the [[Gulf War]] was waged in the [[Middle East]], by a [[United Nations (Earth-616)|U.N.]]-authorized coalition force from thirty-four nations, led by the U.S. and [[United Kingdom]], against [[Saddam Hussein (Earth-616)|Saddam Hussein]]'s [[Iraqi]] forces.{{r|Incredible Hulk Vol 1 387}}

===[[War in Afghanistan]]===
United States President [[George W. Bush (Earth-616)|George W. Bush]] declared a "War on Terror" which saw the U.S. Army invade [[Afghanistan]], beginning the [[War in Afghanistan]]. This led to American forces installing a new government and attempting to stabilize the region, while clashing with the forces of the terrorist organization [[Al-Qaeda (Earth-616)|Al-Qaeda]] and the [[Taliban (Earth-616)|Taliban]].

===[[Iraq War]]===
[[File:Captain America Theatre of War To Soldier On Vol 1 1 Textless.jpg|250px|right|thumb|Cap leading the troops]]
At the same time they also invaded Iraq and oust their leader [[Saddam Hussein (Earth-616)|Saddam Hussein]], beginning the [[Iraq War]]. 

===Modern Age===
The U.S. army has experimented with the [[Gamma Bomb]], which resulted in [[Bruce Banner (Earth-616)|Bruce Banner]] becoming the Hulk.{{r|Incredible Hulk Vol 1 1}}
