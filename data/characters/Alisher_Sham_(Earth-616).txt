| History                 = With the ability to open any lock, Skeleton Ki is a master thief. He was hired to steal the [[Anomaly]] from the [[Rand Corporation (Earth-616)|Rand Corporation]], but was captured as he tried to escape by [[Daniel Rand (Earth-616)|Iron Fist]].{{r|Marvel Comics Presents Vol 1 125}}.

After an Earthquake caused by [[Advanced Idea Mechanics (Earth-616)|A.I.M.]] he escaped.{{r|Marvel Comics Presents Vol 1 126}}

Sometime later, he was imprisoned in the [[Negative Zone]], where he opened the doors to allow [[Blastaar (Earth-616)|Blastaar]] in in return for saving himself. After Blastaar took over the prison, he ordered Skeleton Ki to open the locked portal in the prison which led to the [[Baxter Building]], and that his life depended on it. However, the [[Guardians of the Galaxy (Earth-616)|Guardians of the Galaxy]] warned [[Reed Richards (Earth-616)|Mister Fantastic]] and they shut down the portal. Blastaar seemingly killed Skeleton Ki as a result.{{r|Guardians of the Galaxy Vol 2 10}}
