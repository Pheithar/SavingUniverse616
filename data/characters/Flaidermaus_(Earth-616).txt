| History                 = Flaidermaus was an [[Inhomo supremis|Inhuman]], a offshoot of humanity that was genetically altered by the alien [[Kree]] that gained fantastic powers when exposed to [[Terrigen Mist]]. Flaidermaus, for instance, was covered in fur and had bat-like wings.

He, [[Avius (Earth-616)|Avius]] and [[Pinyon (Earth-616)|Pinyon]] acted as air defense of their homeland of [[Attilan]] during a period it existed in the [[Himalayas|Himalayan Mountains]]. The trio prevented the [[Jonathan Storm (Earth-616)|Human Torch]] from reaching his former girlfriend [[Crystalia Amaquelin (Earth-616)|Crystal]] before he could learn that she had left him for another man.{{r|Fantastic Four #129}}

Flaidermaus' subsequent activities are unknown.
