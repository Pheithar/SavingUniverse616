===[[Strategic Homeland Intervention, Enforcement and Logistics Division (Earth-616)|S.H.I.E.L.D.]]===
'''Olivia Hook''' was a [[Strategic Homeland Intervention, Enforcement and Logistics Division (Earth-616)|S.H.I.E.L.D.]] agent with psionic abilities.

[[Maria Hill (Earth-616)|Director Maria Hill]] ordered her to subdue [[Spider-Man (Peter Parker)|Spider-Man]] when he refused to give up information on the [[Earth-58163|House of M]]. Agent Hook consequently ordered him to sleep. The amount of emotional pain in him saddened her.{{r|New Avengers #19}}

She also battled [[Bennet du Paris (Earth-616)|Exodus]] when his [[Acolytes (Earth-616)|Acolytes]] invaded the [[S.H.I.E.L.D. Helicarrier]], but was taken down quickly along with two other telepaths.{{r|X-Men Annual Vol 3 1}}

==={{m|Howling Commandos Private Military Company|Howling Commandos PMC}}===
After the fall of S.H.I.E.L.D. and the establishment of the [[H.A.M.M.E.R. (Earth-616)|H.A.M.M.E.R.]], Hook joined the {{m|Howling Commandos Private Military Company|Howling Commandos PMC}},{{r|Secret Warriors #1}} and was their sole psi-agent,{{r|Secret Warriors #6}} in charge of their internal security.{{r|Secret Warriors #11}}

After the victory at [[The Dock]] and the desertion of 3,000 agents from H.A.M.M.E.R. into the HCPMC, she was in charge of checking the loyalty of the newly recruited soldier.{{r|Secret Warriors #6}}
