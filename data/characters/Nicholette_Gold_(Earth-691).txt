| History                 = Nicholette "Nikki" Gold was born on the planet [[Mercury (Planet)|Mercury]] in the [[Earth-691|31st Century]]. When she was a child, the [[Badoon]] invaded the solar system and her home planet, killing her parents before her eyes. She managed to flee and was eventually found by the [[Guardians of the Galaxy (Earth-691)|Guardians of the Galaxy]] in an abandoned spacecraft. She joined the Guardians, seeking excitement and adventure after years of solitude.{{r|Marvel Presents #4}} She joined with [[Vance Astro (Earth-691)|Vance Astro]] in a metaphysical union in a successful attempt to use the force of human love to destroy the [[Topographical Man (Earth-691)|Topographical Man]].{{r|Marvel Presents #7}}

Nikki travelled to [[Earth-616|the present]] with the other Guardians and assisted the Avengers in battle against [[Michael Korvac (Earth-691)|Korvac]], where she died fighting him, before being revived by Korvac.{{r|Avengers Vol 1 177}} She travelled to the present again and battled [[Leroy Jackson (Earth-616)|Hammer]] and [[John Anvil (Earth-616)|Anvil]] alongside [[Peter Parker (Earth-616)|Spider-Man]].{{Citation}}

Alongside the Guardians, Nikki later went on a quest to find the lost [[Captain America's Shield|shield of Captain America]], and battled [[Taserface (Earth-691)|Taserface]]. She battled [[Stark (Race)|the Stark]] alongside the other Guardians. She encountered [[Pyreus Kril (Earth-616)|Firelord]]. She battled the super-humanoid team known as [[Force (Earth-691)|Force]] alongside the Guardians.{{Citation}}

During her time on the team, Nikki had a romantic relationship with [[Charlie-27 (Earth-691)|Charlie-27]], though they later amicably separated. She became attracted to newcomer [[Talon (Earth-691)|Talon]], though he simply wished to remain friends.{{Citation}}

| Powers                  = '''[[Mercurians|Mercurian Physiology]]:''' Nikki is a member of a genetically engineered offshoot of humanity whose traits were designed for survival in the harsh conditions of the planet Mercury. As such, she has the ability to see in intense light, and has a high degree of resistance to heat and most types of radiation. Her hair is made of fire and she could potentially burn someone just by touching someone with any part of her body. She would often take complete water baths just to cool her body temperature enough to share physical touch with Charlie-27.
