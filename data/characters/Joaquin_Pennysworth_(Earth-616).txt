Joaquin Pennysworth was a former agent of [[Strategic Homeland Intervention, Enforcement and Logistics Division (Earth-616)|S.H.I.E.L.D]] who had some unfortunate history with [[Kyle Richmond (Earth-616)|Kyle Richmond]], the original Nighthawk (Joaquin's father, J.C. Pennysworth, was a former employee of Richmond's who tried to embezzle funds from him and was the leader of the terrorist group called the [[Sons of the Serpent (Earth-616)|Sons of the Serpent]]).{{r|Defenders Vol 1 #25}}

Despite this, Joaquin agreed to succeed Richmond as [[Nighthawk]] when Richmond was forced to give up the role. He was a member of the [[Defenders (Initiative) (Earth-616)|Defenders]].{{r|Last Defenders Vol 1 6}}
