| History                 = Takimoto was a tracker and assassin working for [[Wolfgang von Strucker (Earth-616)|Baron Strucker's]] Hydra. When three genetic experiments escaped Hydra custody, Takimoto was sent to hunt down the bladed [[Nicos Pelletier (Earth-616)|Guillotine]].{{r|Daredevil Annual #7}}

Guillotine had become involved in a disgraced [[Yakuza (Earth-616)|Yakuza]] boss's efforts to enter the New York City drug trade. Takimoto made an alliance with the [[Frank Castle (Earth-616)|Punisher]] to bring down Guillotine. During their battle with the mutate, however, Takimoto was killed by Guillotine.{{r|Punisher Annual Vol 1 #4}}
