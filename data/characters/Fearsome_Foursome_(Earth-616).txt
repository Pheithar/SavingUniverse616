When the [[Jonathan Storm (Earth-616)|Johnny Storm]] went to trial for setting fire to [[Empire State University]], he was attacked in the courtroom by [[Paibok (Earth-616)|Paibok]] and [[Devos (Earth-616)|Devos]]. Simultaneously, [[Ulysses Klaw (Earth-616)|Klaw]] attempted to have his revenge upon the [[Fantastic Four (Earth-616)|Fantastic Four]] by manipulating [[Tara Richards (Earth-6311)|Huntara]] into joining them. Working together the attacked they FF.{{r|Fantastic Four Vol 1 377}}

However the Fantastic Four four [[Spider-Man (Peter Parker)|Spider-Man]], [[William Baker (Earth-616)|Sandman]], [[Silvija Sablinova (Earth-616)|Silver Sable]] and [[Matthew Murdock (Earth-616)|Daredevil]]. They were winning until the but the [[Avengers (Earth-616)|Avengers]] arrived, they decided to retreat through a portal opened by Huntara. Daredevil attempted to pursue, but the dimension in which he found himself confused his senses, and he had to be pulled out by Spider-Man.{{r|Fantastic Four Vol 1 378}}

On Paibok's starship they planned to destroy the FF at [[Four Freedoms Plaza]], where they lay in wait for the Fantastic Four's return. When the heroes returned, Huntara launched into battle with [[Franklin Richards (Earth-6311)|Psi-Lord]], leading the two of them away from the others. The remaining members of the Fantastic Four were taken out by knock-out gas delivered by Devos and Paibok. The two allies then teleported their captives to Paibok's ship, so that they could turn them over to the [[Skrull Empire]]. After they had disappeared, Klaw came out of hiding, and took the unconscious body of [[Carol Danvers (Earth-616)|Ms Marvel]] prisoner.{{r|Fantastic Four Vol 1 382}}
