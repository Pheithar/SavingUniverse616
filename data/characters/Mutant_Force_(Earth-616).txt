He quickly abandoned them, but the team reorganized into Mutant Force under the [[Jerome Beechman (Earth-616)|Mandrill]].{{r|Defenders #77|78}} Still later, they were employed by the [[Secret Empire (Earth-616)|Secret Empire]] under the leadership of [[Robert Baxter (Earth-616)|Mad-Dog]].{{r|Defenders #125}}

Members of the team re-surfaced later as members of the [[Resistants (Earth-616)|Resistants]].{{r|Captain America Vol 1 343}}

The original five seemed to return to being Mutant Force but were easily apprehended by the [[New Warriors (Earth-616)|New Warriors]].{{r|New Warriors Vol 2 6}} [[Randall Darby (Earth-616)|Shocker]] was later seen in [[Genosha]]. It was presumed that most of the Mutant Force were depowered in the event known as the [[Decimation]]. [[Peter Quinn (Earth-616)|Peeper]] came through it with his powers intact but was killed soon afterward. The rest of the team later resurfaced, their powers also intact, and fought [[Alpha Flight (Space Program) (Earth-616)|Alpha Flight]].{{r|Captain Marvel Vol 9 9}}
