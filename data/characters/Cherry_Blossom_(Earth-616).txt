| History                 = Cherry Blossom is the daughter of a woman who served on a secret all-woman [[Hand (Earth-616)|Hand]] strikeforce known as the [[Nail (Earth-616)|Nail]]. However, her mother was killed on their last mission, along with every other member of the group.

Almost two decades later, the Hand came under the control of the [[Matthew Murdock (Earth-616)|Daredevil]], who decided to reform the Nail. To that end, he found the descendants of various Nail members and offered them a place in the organization. Cherry Blossom was one.{{Citation}}
