| HistoryText             = A female [[Skrulls|Skrull]] alien named [[De'Lila (Earth-616)|De'Lila]] who was an empath, a shape-shifter, and a telepath managed to capture the true [[Fantastic Four (Earth-616)|Fantastic Four]]. Pretending to be [[Susan Storm (Earth-616)|Susan Richards]] she convinced [[James Howlett (Earth-616)|Wolverine]], [[Peter Parker (Earth-616)|Spider-Man]], [[Bruce Banner (Earth-616)|Hulk]], and [[Daniel Ketch (Earth-616)|Ghost Rider]] to form a New Fantastic Four to avenge their "deaths". She sent them to fight the [[Harvey Elder (Earth-616)|Mole Man]] so that she could steal a powerful Skrull device that crash-landed on [[Monster Isle]].{{r|Fantastic Four Vol 1 347}}

The team briefly regrouped with [[Sleepwalker (Earth-616)|Sleepwalker]] instead of Wolverine to fight the reformed [[Sinister Six (Earth-616)|Sinister Six]].{{r|Spider-Man Vol 1 22}}

The original members reunited again as the first [[Secret Defenders (Earth-616)|Secret Defenders]] at [[Stephen Strange (Earth-616)|Dr. Strange]]'s request to bring the [[Jonathan Storm (Earth-616)|Human Torch]] in for arson.{{r|Fantastic Four Vol 1 374}}

When Franklin and Valeria needed help while the FF were away dealing with the latest Kree-Skrull threat, Valeria activated an emergency signal that summoned Spider-Man and Wolverine to aid them, but a message from their father confirmed that the children should consider themselves members of the team as well.{{r|Fantastic Four Vol 6 21|22}}
