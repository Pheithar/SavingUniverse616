===Early Life===
Maximillian Zaran was born in [[United Kingdom]]. Early in his life, he was an agent of the British Secret Service: [[MI6 (Earth-616)|MI6]], but soon chose to become a mercenary and assassin. Training himself thoroughly in martial arts and the use of various kinds of weapons, he became known as a supervillain as well.{{r|Official Handbook of the Marvel Universe Vol 2 1}} He once worked with and started a relationship with [[Zheng Bao Yu (Earth-616)|Fah Lo Suee]], but she soon became disappointed with him so much that she told him that she did not care for him because he was weak.{{Citation}}

Zaran sought out different assignments to gain back Suee's love and trust again.{{Citation}}

===Shang-Chi===
His first battle against a super-hero was against [[Shang-Chi (Earth-616)|Shang-Chi]], the Master of Kung Fu (and Suee's half-brother), who easily defeated him. He later again battled and defeated Shang-Chi.{{r|Master of Kung Fu Vol 1 76|89}}

===Batroc's Brigade===
He joined the [[Batroc's Brigade (Earth-616)|Batroc's Brigade]] along with [[Georges Batroc (Earth-616)|Batroc]] and [[Ferdinand Lopez (Earth-616)|Machete]] to steal [[Captain America's Shield|Captain America's shield]], but they failed.{{r|Captain America Vol 1 302|304}}

===West Coast Avengers===
He teamed with [[Douglas Scott (Earth-616)|Razor-Fist]] and [[Lancaster Sneed (Earth-616)|Shockwave]] to attack the [[Avengers West Coast (Earth-616)|Avengers West Coast]] on behalf of the [[S.H.I.E.L.D.]] [[Deltite]] to retrieve the Blood Tong.{{r|West Coast Avengers Vol 2 11}} 

[[Buck Chisholm (Earth-616)|Trick Shot]] then hired Batroc and his Brigade to take out the Avenger; [[Clinton Barton (Earth-616)|Hawkeye]].{{r|Solo Avengers Vol 1 3}}

===Nomad===
Zaran was hired by [[Giscard Epurer (Earth-616)|Giscard Epurer]] to attack [[Jack Monroe (Earth-616)|Nomad]].{{r|Nomad Vol 2 18|22}}

===The Architect===
[[Architect (Shining Ones) (Earth-616)|Architect]] offered big money to the first assassin that could kill him.{{r|Elektra Vol 2 7}}

===Albino===
[[Augusta_Seger (Earth-616)|Albino]], disguised as a computer programmer in Virtual War Themes, asked Hawkeye for help in protecting a computer virus capable of creating chaos throughout the world from falling into the wrong hands. Hawkeye escorted her to her place of business and was attacked by Batroc's Brigade. Hawkeye managed to defeat Batroc's Brigade and found a mask with Augusta Seger's face on it.{{r|Hawkeye: Earth's Mightiest Marksman Vol 1 1}}

===Bloodstone Hunt===
[[File:Maximillian Zaran (Earth-616) from Wolverine Vol 2 158 0001.jpg|right|thumb|"Zaran"]]
During the legendary [[Bloodstone]] Hunt, Zaran became good friends with fellow mercenary Batroc and fought Captain America but lost once more.{{r|Captain America Vol 1 357|362}}{{r|Captain America Vol 1 411}}

===Great Lakes Avengers===
[[Grasshopper]] came across Batroc's Brigade breaking into a building and decided to try and stop them. The [[Great Lakes Avengers (Earth-616)|Great Lakes Avengers]] rush to aid Grasshopper with the robbery and ask him to join the team, Grasshopper accepts shortly before Zaran throws a dagger at his head.{{r|G.L.A. ===R.A.I.D.===
Later, Zaran traveled to England as part of terrorist group [[Radically Advanced Ideas in Destruction (Earth-616)|R.A.I.D.]]'s attack against the city of London. Zaran and [[Mariano Lopez (Earth-616)|Machete]] were tasked with assassinating the Houses of Parliament, but they were stopped by an ad hoc group of superheroes led by [[Joseph Chapman (Earth-616)|Union Jack]].{{r|Union Jack Vol 2 1}}

===Civil War/Initiative===
Escaping jail and facing the Thunderbolts, Zaran was forced to join said superhero group during the [[Civil War]] event.{{r|New Thunderbolts Vol 1 8}} Zaran has been identified as one of the 142 registered superheroes who are part of the Initiative program.{{r|Thunderbolts Vol 1 107}}
