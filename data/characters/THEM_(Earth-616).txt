'''THEM''' was the central committee [[Wolfgang von Strucker (Earth-616)|Baron von Strucker]] set up to direct operations of two of [[Hydra (Earth-616)|Hydra]]'s subsidiaries: [[Advanced Idea Mechanics (Earth-616)|Advanced Idea Mechanics]] and the [[Secret Empire (Earth-616)|Secret Empire]].{{r|Strange Tales Vol 1 149}}{{r|All-New Official Handbook of the Marvel Universe A to Z Vol 1 5}}

During their tenure, THEM helped [[Norbert Ebersol (Earth-616)|Fixer]] develop advanced technology,{{r|Strange Tales Vol 1 142}} revived the Red Skull from his 1945 suspended animation, developed the [[Cosmic Cube]], almost detonated [[New York City]] with their chemical bomb [[Inferno 42]], and made several attempts to eliminate [[Steven Rogers (Earth-616)|Captain America]] and [[Nicholas Fury (Earth-616)|Nick Fury]].{{r|Tales of Suspense Vol 1 75|79}}
