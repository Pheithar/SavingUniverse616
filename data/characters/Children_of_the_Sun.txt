| HistoryText             = A race of humanoid creatures created by [[Ex Nihilo (Earth-616)|Ex Nihilo]] in his attempt to evolve Earth.{{r|Avengers Vol 5 4}} During his trip to the [[Savage Land]], they were founded by [[Marcus Milton (Earth-13034)|Hyperion]], that began spending a deal of his time caring for and educating them becoming their "father".{{r|Avengers Vol 5 #12|13}}

This species is totally self-sustained, they don't need to eat, breath or sleep, and develop at an incredible speed.{{r|Avengers Vol 5 12}}

The Children of the Sun later helped [[Roberto Da Costa (Earth-616)|Sunspot]] investigate the [[Glossary:Incursion|Incursions]] and create a countermeasure for them.{{r|Avengers Vol 5 35}}

After the restoration of the universe thanks to Reed Richards, the Children of the Sun started working for Avengers Idea Mechanics. {{r|New Avengers Vol 4 10}}
