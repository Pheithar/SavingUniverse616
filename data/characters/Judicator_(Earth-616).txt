===Contest of Champions===
The Judicator took part in a contest of champions between the [[Elders of the Universe (Earth-616)|Elders of the Universe]] to decide who would keep the [[Neutronium|Iso-8]] that was now abundant after the rebirth of the multiverse, her champions lost and she was eliminated from the contest.{{r|Contest of Champions Vol 1 6}}
