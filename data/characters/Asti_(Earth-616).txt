A demonic creature that has served as a minion of [[Dormammu (Earth-616)|Dormammu]], Asti was once set upon [[Stephen Strange (Earth-616)|Doctor Strange]] who was able to defeat the creature.{{r|Strange Tales #144}}

Later, during the ''[[Avengers/Defenders War]]'', Dormammu sent Asti to collect the completed [[Evil Eye]] from the two hero teams in order merge the [[Dark Dimension]] with [[Earth]]'s dimension. While Asti succeeded in its task, Dormammu's plot ultimately failed.{{r|Defenders #10}}

Asti was later recruited by the [[Reed Richards (Earth-1610)|Maker]] to join his [[New Revengers (Earth-616)|New Revengers]].{{r|New Avengers Vol 4 7}}
