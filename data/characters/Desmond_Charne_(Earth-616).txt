===Origin===
[[File:Desmond Charne (Earth-616) from Superior Foes of Spider-Man Vol 1 3 0001.jpg|thumb|left|Flashback of Charne's life]]
Desmond Charne was a holographic technician who always wanted to become a costumed criminal. He and his gang began robbing weddings including that of [[Elizabeth Brant (Earth-616)|Betty Brant]] and [[Edward Leeds (Earth-616)|Ned Leeds]] He attacked the party to rob all the guests of their valuables. [[Spider-Man (Peter Parker)|Peter Parker]] managed to slip away, by snapping off the lights with a strand of webbing from one of his web-shooters, and switch to [[Spider-Man]]. After a lengthy battle, Spider-Man is finally able to defeat the Mirage by dropping a chandelier on him.{{r|Amazing Spider-Man Vol 1 156}}{{r|Amazing Spider-Man Vol 1 181}}

===Daredevil===
Mirage also tried to attack the [[Benjamin Grimm (Earth-616)|Thing]] when he was in Hospital but was defeated by [[Matthew Murdock (Earth-616)|Daredevil]].{{r|Marvel Two-In-One Vol 1 96}}

===Scourge===
He later went to a meeting of costumed criminals at the Bar with no name, where he spoke with [[Hellen Feliciano (Earth-616)|Letha]] of the [[Grapplers (Earth-616)|Grapplers]].{{r|Captain America Vol 1 319}} Scourge was disguised as Jake the Bartender and killed Mirage along with all the other costumed criminals there.{{r|Captain America Vol 1 320}}{{r|Amazing Spider-Man Vol 1 364}}

===Proto-Husk===
[[Arnim Zola (Earth-616)|Arnim Zola]] created proto-husk version of Mirage, but it was killed by [[Wade Wilson (Earth-616)|Deadpool]].{{r|Deadpool Vol 3 0}}

===Resurrection===
Charne was one of the eighteen dead villains resurrected by the [[Parker Robbins (Earth-616)|Hood]] in order to kill the [[Frank Castle (Earth-616)|Punisher]].{{r|Punisher Vol 8 5|6}} Charne's powers of illusion were also enhanced. [[Davida DeVito (Earth-616)|Lascivious]]  demonstrated her new emotion based powers on him, making him lick her boots.{{r|Punisher Vol 8 7}} Charne disguised himself and a number of the criminals as the [[Avengers (Earth-616)|Avengers]], out to kill the Punisher. The Punisher uncovered the ruse when he knocked out Charne disguised as Captain America and captured him. He interrogated Charne about the Hood's plans, and left him with a grenade in his mouth. Mirage escaped and [[Hellen Feliciano (Earth-616)|Letha]] removed the grenade in time. Letha ordered [[Achille DiBacco (Earth-616)|Bird-Man]] to take him to safety.{{r|Punisher Vol 8 8}} Charne was tracked by the Punisher to a  nightclub where he interrogated about [[Microchip]]'s whereabouts, but Charne was shot by a female assassin.{{r|Punisher: In the Blood Vol 1 2}} He actually survived and spent some months in a coma before awakening.{{r|Superior Foes of Spider-Man Vol 1 3}}

===Supervillains Anonymous===
Mirage later attended a support group called Supervillains Anonymous that was held at a church and also attended by [[Frederick Myers (Earth-616)|Boomerang]], [[Hippo (Earth-616)|Hippo]], [[Roger Gocking (Earth-616)|Porcupine]], [[Lester Verde (Earth-616)|Dr. Bong]], [[Norton Fester (Earth-616)|Looter]], [[Maxwell Markham (Earth-616)|Grizzly]], and others.{{r|Superior Foes of Spider-Man Vol 1 11|13}}

===Sinister Sixteen===
Mirage was recruited into the [[Leland Owlsley (Earth-616)|Owl]]'s [[Sinister Six (Earth-616)|Sinister Sixteen]]. The group was assembled to distract the [[Dmitri Smerdyakov (Earth-616)|Chameleon]]'s forces while Boomerang stole from him. During the battle between the group and the Chameleon's men, Mirage was knocked out and impersonated by the Chameleon, who in turn was knocked out by Boomerang when he saw through the ruse.{{r|Superior Foes of Spider-Man Vol 1 15}} Charne was pushed off a roof by Boomerang after foolishly agreeing to help him steal a painting.{{r|Superior Foes of Spider-Man Vol 1 17}}

===Dead No More===
Mirage died from his fall, but was later brought back to life by the [[Benjamin Reilly (Earth-616)|Jackal]], and became one of his employees at [[New U Technologies (Earth-616)|New U Technologies]].{{r|Prowler Vol 2 1}} Mirage then died from clone degeneration,{{r|Prowler Vol 2 5}} only to be brought back to [[Earth]] despite still being deceased when [[Stephen Strange (Earth-616)|Doctor Strange]] restored [[Las Vegas]].{{r|Doctor Strange: Damnation Vol 1 1}}
