===Early Life===
Michael "Man Mountain" Marko was a street thug living in [[New York City]], on day like any other he and his gang spotted someone to harass. The ordinary looking teenager was none other then [[Spider-Man (Peter Parker)|Peter Parker]]. Peter was distracted by his [[Maybelle Parker (Earth-616)|Aunt May]]'s illness, just knocking Marko away like he was nothing. It was from that day that Marko began his quest to gain powers.{{r|Web of Spider-Man Vol 1 82}} His first step was to become bodybuilder and to take work as a bodyguard.{{r|Amazing Spider-Man Vol 1 73}}

===Maggia===
[[File:Michael_Marko_(Earth-616)_from_Amazing_Spider-Man_Vol_2_73.jpg|left|180px|thumb|Working for the Maggia]]
Man Mountain Marko joined the [[Maggia (Earth-616)|Maggia]]. They gave him his wish and had he strength body enhanced through genetic engineering. Using his new found strength he became the  loyal lieutenant to [[Silvio Manfredi (Earth-616)|Silvermane]]. He was sent by Silvermane to steal the [[Lifeline Tablet]] taken by the [[Herman Schultz (Earth-616)|Shocker]]. He was confronted and defeated by Spider-Man.{{r|Amazing Spider-Man Vol 1 73}}

He then threatened [[Curtis Connors (Earth-616)|Curt Connors]] until the scientist agreed to work with [[Louis Wilson (Earth-616)|Louis Wilson]] to crack the ancient tablet's secret code.{{r|Amazing Spider-Man Vol 1 74}}

When Silvermane drank the elixir of life, made with the tablet's formula, Marko was at first deceived by [[Caesar Cicero (Earth-616)|Caesar Cicero]] into thinking that the young man was an impostor; nevertheless, when Spider-Man busted in the Galby Building, he tried to protect his master, but he was easily defeated again.{{r|Amazing Spider-Man Vol 1 75}}

===Heroes for Hire===
Marko was sent after [[Benjamin Donovan (Earth-616)|Big Ben Donovan]], who went to the [[Heroes for Hire (Power Man & Iron Fist) (Earth-616)|Heroes for Hire]] for help. He crashed into the office followed by Marko. He managed to knock out [[Luke Cage (Earth-616)|Luke Cage]] when their fight exposed live electrical wires which Marko used electrocute him. He took them both to Caesar Cicero, who wanted Donovan to reveal the location of stolen drugs while preparing Cage to be buried alive.{{r|Power Man and Iron Fist Vol 1 61}} However Iron Fist arrived and defeated Marko and saved Cage from his fate.{{r|Power Man and Iron Fist Vol 1 62}}

Marko was sent to free [[Hammerhead (Joseph) (Earth-616)|Hammerhead]] from a prison transport protected by the Heroes for Hire. Marko along with the [[Edward Lavell (Earth-616)|Eel]] were easily matched against the heroes. However Luke and Danny traded partners, pitting the Eel against Luke, and Man Mountain against Iron Fist, the Heroes for Hire managed to beat their foes. Hammerhead crashed into the prison wall and was arrested.{{r|Power Man and Iron Fist Vol 1 92}}

He teamed up with [[Tilda Johnson (Earth-616)|Nightshade]], [[Thomas Stuart (Earth-616)|Stiletto]], [[Timothy Stuart (Earth-616)|Discus]], and the Eel to kidnap [[Jeryn Hogarth (Earth-616)|Jeryn Hogarth]] daughter but were stopped by the Heroes for Hire.{{r|Power Man and Iron Fist Vol 1 110}}

Marko obsession with becoming more powerful lead to him using horse steroids. He collected protection money for the Maggia when he went a bit crazy. He trashed an entire bar with Spider-Man inside. [[Benjamin Urich (Earth-616)|Ben Urich]] told Spidey that Marko took off after kidnapping a kid. Spider-Man learned the truth about the steroids. He tracked Marko to his home but he got the the better of Spider-Man as they fight at first but he tires and Spider-Man fights back and knocks him out. Meanwhile, the 'kidnapped' boy was just a fan of Marko's who wanted to be as big as him.{{r|Web of Spider-Man Vol 1 82}}
 
===Jessica Jones===
Marko was hired by Mr. [[R. Zoumas (Earth-616)|Zoumas]] to kill the private detective [[Jessica Jones (Earth-616)|Jessica Jones]].{{r|Alias #4}} He attempted to strangle her, but Jessica easily defeated him. She dumped the unconscious body of Marko at the feet of Mr. Zoumas during his golf game. They were both the arrested by [[Clay Quartermain (Earth-1610)|Agent Clay Quartermain]] of [[Strategic Homeland Intervention, Enforcement and Logistics Division (Earth-616)|S.H.I.E.L.D.]].{{r|Alias #5}}

===Dazzler===
Marko tried to quit his life of crime and became a relatively successful singer, even touring [[Europe]]. On a flight back to the [[United States]], Marko got drunk and harassed the other passengers. [[Alison Blaire (Earth-616)|Dazzler]] was also on the same flight. She used her powers against him to no avail, threatening a stewardess he forced her to sing. The song did not calm him, so she used her powers again knocking him out.{{r|X-Men: Manifest Destiny Vol 1 5}}

===Civil War===
Marko joined Hammerhead's group who planned to take advantage of the upheaval during the [[Civil War]]. However their base was raided by [[Iron Man (Anthony "Tony" Stark)|Iron Man]] and [[Strategic Homeland Intervention, Enforcement and Logistics Division (Earth-616)|S.H.I.E.L.D.]].{{r|Civil War: War Crimes Vol 1 1}}

===Dark Reign===
During [[Dark Reign]], [[Norman Osborn (Earth-616)|Norman Osborn]] became head of [[H.A.M.M.E.R. (Earth-616)|H.A.M.M.E.R.]]. He offered Marko a job as an [[Bureau of Alcohol, Tobacco, Firearms and Explosives (Earth-616)|ATF]] agent. He was tasked with hunting down the [[Agents of Atlas (Earth-616)|Agents of Atlas]]. However he was no match for [[Aquaria Neptunia (Earth-616)|Namora]] and was teleported to a desert by the [[Robert Grayson (Earth-616)|Uranian]]. At another mission to the [[Temple of Atlas]] one of the ATF members went rogue, but when Marko confronted him, he revealed that he was [[James Woo (Earth-616)|Jimmy Woo]]. The Agents of Atlas attacked and Marko fled, frightened of Namora. He hid but was found by [[Mr. Lao (Earth-616)|Mr. Lao]] the giant dragon who swallowed Marko whole.{{r|Agents of Atlas Vol 2 1}}

He was later revealed to be alive and working for [[Frederick Myers (Earth-616)|Boomerang]]'s [[Sinister Six (Earth-616)|Sinister Sixteen]].{{r|Superior Foes of Spider-Man Vol 1 12}} 

===Fist===
[[File:Spider-Man 2099 Vol 3 8 Textless.jpg|right|thumb]]
Man Mountain Marko was hired by [[Cecelia Monroe (Earth-616)|Cecelia Monroe]] to guard her [[Tempest Monroe (Earth-616)|daughter]]'s hospital room. Tempest's [[Miguel O'Hara (Earth-928)|boyfriend]] attempted to bribe his way into the room and Marko responded by throwing him into the stairwell. Marko checked the stairwell and assumed Miguel had took off, but when he returned to Tempest's room, he found Miguel in his Spider-Man guise. Believing him to be the original, Marko attacks Miguel. Their fight ended when Marko threatened to kill the babies in the nursery unless he left. Marko returned to the room to find it empty, realizing that their battle was just a diversion.{{r|Spider-Man 2099 Vol 3 8}}

Marko was tracked down by Spider-Man, who was looking for information on Cecelia. He claimed he never heard of her, until Spider-Man started breaking his fingers, then he eagerly told him her [[18 Mountain Avenue|home address]]. He briefly got the upper hand, but Spider-Man ended up beating him to a pulp. After Spidey left, Marko swore he wouldn't lose to him again.{{r|Spider-Man 2099 Vol 3 9}} He joined up with [[Fist (Earth-616)|The Fist]] and they augmented his powers. He accompanied [[Tyler Stone (Earth-928)|Tyler Stone]] to [[Parker Industries Medical]], where he kept Spider-Man busy while Tyler convinced Tempest to leave with them to [[Blackthorne Prison]].{{r|Spider-Man 2099 Vol 3 20}} When Spider-Man arrived at the prison, Marko rushed him only to realize Spider-Man didn't want to battle. Miguel paid him off and two bonded after Miguel revealed that Tempest was carrying his [[Gabriel O'Hara II (Earth-616)|son]]. Watching their encounter, Stone mind controlled Marko to attack Miguel. With the help of his halo-assistant, [[LYrate Lifeform Approximation (Earth-928)|Lyla]], Spider-Man kept Marko under the [[East River]] until he passed out.{{r|Spider-Man 2099 Vol 3 25}}
