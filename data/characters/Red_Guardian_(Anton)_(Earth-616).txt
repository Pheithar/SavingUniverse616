| History                 = The seventh Red Guardian, Anton was a member of the [[Winter Guard (Earth-616)|Winter Guard]]. Anton claimed to be an engineer and one of the original [[Crimson Dynamo]] pilots. He eventually transferred his mind to an [[Life-Model Decoy]] in order to be stronger than a human.{{r|Hulk: Winter Guard #1}}

The Winter Guard and [[James Rhodes (Earth-616)|War Machine]] helped defend Russia during the [[Secret Invasion|Skrull Invasion]].{{r|Iron Man: Director of S.H.I.E.L.D. Vol 1 #34|#35}}

When the [[Protectorate (Earth-616)|Protectorate]] returned to Earth, they revealed that long-time member [[Fantasia (Earth-616)|Fantasia]] was actually a [[Dire Wraith]] all along. They went to the former Winter Guard base, where she was waiting. While they were hiding in the building, Anton attempted to create a plan, but was decapitated by a Dire Wraith. His head was kept in storage, seemingly still alive, by [[Galina Nemirovsky (Earth-616)|Galina Nemirovsky]], planning to use him to destroy the Winter Guard.{{r|Darkstar and the Winter Guard #3}}
