| History                 = An assassin in the employ of terrorist organization [[Hydra (Earth-616)|Hydra]], Bull's Eye was ordered to murder [[Nicholas Fury (Earth-616)|Nicholas Fury]], director of Hydra's enemy [[Strategic Homeland Intervention, Enforcement and Logistics Division (Earth-616)|S.H.I.E.L.D.]]. Using a sniper rifle, Bull's Eye shot down Fury while he was at a rock festival with his date. Immediately after that, S.H.I.E.L.D. agents found Bull's Eye and shot him down dead.{{r|Nick Fury, Agent of SHIELD Vol 1 15}} Only later S.H.I.E.L.D. revealed that Fury had not been killed - Bull's Eye only shot down a [[Life-Model Decoy]] robot that Fury had sent as his doppleganger. Fury chose to keep his survival a secret except to his second-in-command [[Timothy Dugan (Earth-616)|Timothy Dugan]], to get a chance against other enemies.{{r|Avengers Vol 1 72}}
