| History                 = Magdalene served on her world's [[Avengers (Earth-9201)|Avengers]] until the team was destroyed through the manipulations of [[Dane Whitman (Earth-374)|Proctor]].  She felt a strong attraction to her savior, unaware he had caused her world's [[Sersi (Earth-9201)|Sersi]] to go insane.{{r|Avengers #344}}{{r|Avengers #375}}

However, when their next mission ended with [[Philip Javert (Earth-921)|Swordsman]] joining, she soon fell in love with him instead.{{r|Avengers #344}}

When the Gatherers arrived on [[Earth-616]], Magdalene's counterpart [[Marissa Darrow (Earth-616)|Marissa Darrow]] was murdered and 'gathered' by Proctor.{{r|Avengers #348}} As Javert began to lose trust in Proctor, so did Magdalene. She eventually left the Gatherers with Swordsman and became an associate member of the Avengers.{{r|Avengers #363|364}}

She and Javert assisted the Avengers on missions although they still felt they did not belong on this world. They declined Avengers membership and sought to find a new home for themselves. With the assistance of [[Anthony Stark (Earth-616)|Iron Man]], Magdalene used her lance to open a portal to a new dimension; both left and sought to make a new life for themselves.{{r|Avengers/Squadron Supreme Annual Vol 1 '98}}
