In the mountains in Iraq, [[Nikola Tesla (Earth-616)|Nikola Tesla]] finds the three capsules where the three members of the Last Caliphate rest; he tells Mina, calling them those who mark the way and who are slowly waking up from a sleep of centuries.{{r|S.H.I.E.L.D.: Infinity Vol 1 1}}

The three are awakened by Nikola Tesla, [[Howard Stark (Earth-616)|Howard Stark]] and [[Nathaniel Richards (Earth-616)|Nathaniel Richards]]. Everyone went to the [[Immortal City]] and announced to [[Michelangelo Simoni (Earth-616)|Michelangelo]] that they will travel to the end of human history not knowing whether to save the world or see it burn. Nikola Tesla tells his son [[Leonid (Earth-616)|Leonid]] that the three represent the branches of time, what is, what has been before and what will be., Then embarking all on the [[Human Machine]] they went into the future facing three scenarios where they would always clash with [[Isaac Newton (Earth-616)|Isaac Newton]].{{r|S.H.I.E.L.D. Vol 2 4}}

In all three scenarios, the three brothers perished in the fight with Newton and the [[Star Child (Earth-616)|Star Child]].{{r|S.H.I.E.L.D. by Hickman & Weaver Vol 1 5}}

Leonid using the Human Machine with his powers defeated Newton and restored reality by bringing the three brothers of the Last Caliphate back to life.{{r|S.H.I.E.L.D. by Hickman & Weaver Vol 1 6}}
