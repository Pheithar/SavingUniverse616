| HistoryText             = The Euroforce is a team of European superheroes and a merger of scientific investigations team called [[EuroLab (Earth-616)|EuroLab]] and the military unit codenamed [[Task Force (Earth-616)|Task Force]].{{r|Europa Vol 1 3}}

Euroforce later came under the leadership of [[Dane Whitman (Earth-616)|Black Knight]].{{r|Avengers World Vol 1 8}}

===[[Secret Empire (Event)|Secret Empire]]===
When [[Steven Rogers (Earth-616)|Captain America]] established a [[Hydra (Earth-616)|Hydra]] Empire in the [[United States of America]], Hydra splinter groups started to form all over the world and several superhero teams, such as the Euroforce, were gathered to stop Hydra from going any further.{{r|Captain America: Steve Rogers Vol 1 18}} During one of their attacks, most of the team ended up in prison, but they were all freed by the [[Champions of Europe (Earth-616)|Champions of Europe]] later.{{r|U.S.Avengers Vol 1 9}}
