===Origin===
A clone of [[Andrea von Strucker (Earth-616)|Andrea von Strucker]] created by [[Arnim Zola (Earth-616)|Arnim Zola]] and commissioned by [[Andreas von Strucker (Earth-616)|Andreas von Strucker]].{{r|Thunderbolts #122}}

===Thunderbolts===
[[File:Andreas and Andrea von Strucker (Earth-616) from Thunderbolts Vol 1 122 0001.jpg|left|thumb]]
Andreas took her along when the [[Thunderbolts (Earth-616)|Thunderbolts]] went to [[Washington, D.C.]] to deal with the [[Skrull Invasion]].{{r|Thunderbolts #122}}

===Skrull Infiltrator===
She was initially feared to be a Skrull, but when [[Karla Sofen (Earth-616)|Moonstone]] attempted to cut a deal with her for part of the conquered planet, she attacked Moonstone.{{r|Thunderbolts #123}} Preparing to finish off Moonstone, she was stabbed from behind by [[Bullseye (Lester) (Earth-616)|Bullseye]], which proved fatal. It was confirmed that she was not a Skrull.{{r|Thunderbolts #124}}

| Powers                  = * '''[[Energy Blasts|Sympathetic Bio-Blasting]]''': As a [[clone]] of [[Andrea von Strucker (Earth-616)|Andrea von Strucker]] she shares her powers. Her beams can atomize a human.{{r|Thunderbolts #122}}
