| History                 = <!-- ===...===
 -->'''Karl Striklan''' was an [[United States Marine Corps (Earth-616)|U.S. Marine]] but was dishonorably discharged for unspecified reasons. Striklan later became a cop but was dismissed for excessive brutality and violent towards criminals. He even joined [[Hydra (Earth-616)|Hydra]] for a short period and in that time he battled [[Matthew Murdock (Earth-616)|Daredevil]].{{r|Daredevil Annual #7}}

Striklan took up the name '''Crippler''', quit Hydra and went freelance. He eventually joined [[Silvija Sablinova (Earth-616)|Silver Sable]]'s International mercenary team [[Wild Pack (Earth-616)|Wild Pack]].{{r|Silver Sable and the Wild Pack #2}}
