| Overview                = '''Kyle Jinadu''' is the husband of [[Jean-Paul Beaubier (Earth-616)|Northstar]] and the long-time PR rep for [[Team Northstar Extreme Snowsports (Earth-616)|Team Northstar Extreme Snowsports]].

| History                 = Kyle Jinadu has been a trusted friend of [[Jeanne-Marie Beaubier (Earth-616)|Jeanne-Marie Beaubier]] for ages. Eventually, she asked him to become the public relations representative for [[Team Northstar Extreme Snowsports (Earth-616)|Team Northstar Extreme Snowsports]] in hopes of keeping her brother, [[Jean-Paul Beaubier (Earth-616)|Northstar]], out of trouble,{{r|Uncanny X-Men Vol 1 508}}{{r|Nation X Vol 1 2}} only to find out he would someday be her brother-in-law.{{r|Astonishing X-Men Vol 3 51}}

[[File:Jean-Paul Beaubier (Earth-616) and Kyle Jinadu (Earth-616) from Uncanny X-Men Vol 1 508 001.jpg|left|thumb|200px|Jean-Paul & Kyle together at Mt. Shasta.]]

Since they started dating, Kyle has continued to manage Jean-Paul's brand and coordinate shows, audio-visual, and personal relations. When [[James Howlett (Earth-616)|Wolverine]] invited Northstar to join the X-Men and relocate to [[San Francisco]], Kyle was supportive.{{r|Uncanny X-Men Vol 1 508}} However, the long distance put a strain on their relationship. Following an overwhelming visit to [[Utopia (X-Men Base)|Utopia]], Kyle and Jean-Paul had to discuss how Kyle could never become a part of the ''superhero world''. Thankfully, the couple decided to make some compromises for each other and stayed together.{{r|Nation X Vol 1 2}}

[[File:Jean-Paul Beaubier (Earth-616) and Kyle Jinadu (Earth-616) from Alpha Flight Vol 4 6 001.jpg|left|thumb|200px|Northstar rescuing a brainwashed Kyle]]

While Northstar was impromptuly assisting [[Alpha Flight (Earth-616)|Alpha Flight]], Kyle was trying to get photos of him for a press release. Unfortunately, Kyle was part of a crowd that came under the mental control of the [[Kara Killgrave (Earth-616)|Purple Woman]].{{r|Alpha Flight Vol 4 0.1}} Not long after this incident, Kyle's apartment was attacked by [[Box Armor Model 1|Box robot drones]], injuring and nearly killing Kyle. During surgery, Kyle's body was abducted by agents from [[Department H (Earth-616)|Department H]].{{r|Alpha Flight Vol 4 1|2}} Under the direction of the [[Eshu (Earth-616)|Master of the World]], Kyle's body was taken apart and rebuilt using [[Plodex]] technology.{{r|Alpha Flight Vol 4 4}} As part of being rebuilt, Kyle was placed under some type of advanced effects of the Purple Woman, and used as a spokesmen against Northstar and Alpha Flight.{{r|Alpha Flight Vol 4 6}} Kyle was eventually rescued and freed from the Purple Woman's influence, but not before fighting against Alpha Flight.{{r|Alpha Flight Vol 4 8}}

[[File:Astonishing X-Men Vol 3 51 Wraparound Textless.jpg|right|thumb|300px|The wedding of [[Jean-Paul Beaubier (Earth-616)|Jean-Paul]] & Kyle.]]

After trying making their long distance relationship work for too long, Kyle and Northstar finally moved into an apartment together in [[Hell's Kitchen]] while Northstar continued his work with [[James Howlett (Earth-616)|Wolverine]]'s [[X-Men (Earth-616)|X-Men]].{{r|Astonishing X-Men Vol 3 48}} As soon as they unpacked, Kyle was abducted by the [[Marauders (Earth-616)|Marauders]] and then mind-controlled by a psychically-infected [[Xi'an Coy Manh (Earth-616)|Karma]]. Relieved that he was safe, Northstar proposed to Kyle.{{r|Astonishing X-Men Vol 3 48|50}} One week later, they were married in [[Central Park]] with their friends and family in attendance.{{r|Astonishing X-Men Vol 3 51|52}} Despite being legally married and Kyle being a U.S. citizen, I.C.E. began investigating Northstar in an attempt to deport him.{{r|Astonishing X-Men Vol 3 56}}{{r|Astonishing X-Men Vol 3 59}}{{r|Astonishing X-Men Vol 3 68}}

After their wedding, the couple spent their honeymoon in [[Paris (France)|Paris]], [[France]]. Unfortunately, the [[Friends of Humanity (Earth-616)|Friends of Humanity]] began targeting human family members of mutants and attempted to assassinate Kyle. The [[X-Men (Earth-616)|X-Men]] and Northstar were able to thwart the attack, but it left Kyle shaken.{{r|Astonishing X-Men Annual Vol 1 1}}

During the "snowpocalypse" caused by [[Robert Drake (Earth-616)|Iceman]],{{r|Astonishing X-Men Vol 3 62|65}} Northstar and Kyle went to [[Canada]] to help their families and other Canadians.{{r|Astonishing X-Men Vol 3 66}}

Kyle and Northstar were among the many guests invited to [[Wade Wilson (Earth-616)|Deadpool]] and [[Shiklah (Earth-616)|Shiklah]]'s wedding reception.{{r|Deadpool Vol 5 27}} As a couple, they attended [[Robert Drake (Earth-616)|Iceman]]'s coming out/going away party (before he decided ''not'' to move to [[Los Angeles|L.A.]]).{{r|Iceman Vol 3 9|10}} They also went to Iceman's surprise birthday party at [[Coffee A Go-Go|Java A Go-Go]].{{r|Uncanny X-Men: Winters End Vol 1 1}}

[[File:Kyle Jinadu (Earth-616) and Jean-Paul Beaubier (Earth-616) from X-Factor Vol 4 6 001.jpg|right|thumb|200px|Settling in on [[Krakoa (Earth-616)|Krakoa]].]]

When the mutant nation of [[Krakoa (Earth-616)|Krakoa]] was revealed to the world,{{r|House of X Vol 1 1}} Kyle moved with [[Jean-Paul Beaubier (Earth-616)|Northstar]] to live in the [[Boneyard (Krakoa)|Boneyard]] on Krakoa.{{r|X-Factor Vol 4 1}} Because [[Jubilation Lee (Earth-616)|Jubilee]] wants [[Shogo Lee (Earth-616)|Shogo]] to have some "positive human influences" in his life, Kyle is occasionally recruited to babysit him.{{r|X-Factor Vol 4 7}}

Along with [[Jean-Paul Beaubier (Earth-616)|Northstar]] and the rest of [[X-Factor (Earth-616)|X-Factor]], Kyle attended the inaugural [[Hellfire Gala]] of the Krakoan era on [[Mykines]].{{r|Excalibur Vol 4 21}}{{r|Marauders Vol 1 21}}{{r|New Mutants Vol 4 19}} At the Gala, Kyle chided [[Kurt Wagner (Earth-616)|Nightcrawler]]'s procreative interpretation of Krakoa's first law for being too heteronormative.{{r|Way of X Vol 1 3}} 

Kyle tried to comfort [[Thomas Shepherd (Earth-616)|Tommy Shepherd]], who hadn't left his [[Wanda Maximoff (Earth-616)|mother]]'s side since she was put in the Boneyard, but found a strange vegetation had engulfed her body. Neither knew what to do at this point; Tommy wished he could talk to Billy but [[Last Annihilation|he couldn't be reached]].{{r|X-Men: The Trial of Magneto Vol 1 1}} The next day when he was returning from work he was taken hostage by [[Max Eisenhardt (Earth-616)|Magneto]] as leverage to get the [[Avengers (Earth-616)|Avengers]] to leave. Northstar ordered them to comply saying none of them were were worth putting Kyle's life at risk but they were interrupted when the Scarlet Witch appeared seemingly alive and well.{{r|X-Men: The Trial of Magneto Vol 1 2}}
