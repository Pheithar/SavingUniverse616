===Cohen===
{| class="article-table"
|[[File:Agent Cohen (Earth-616) from The Pulse Vol 1 8 001.png|150px]]
|'''Appearances:''' {{cl|The Pulse Vol 1 8}}

[[Jessica Jones (Earth-616)|Jessica Jones]] wakes to find herself in a hangar with a woman calling herself Special Agent Cohen, who claims to be an empath. Cohen calls her associates to inform them that Jessica is awake, and moments later, four Hydra agents arrive.
Agent Cohen explains that [[Luke Cage (Earth-616)|Luke]] was attacked because of his part in [[Nicholas Fury (Earth-616)|Nick Fury]]'s "Secret War," and assures Jessica that Hydra can find Luke for her no matter where he has been taken. She tells Jessica that [[Strategic Homeland Intervention, Enforcement and Logistics Division (Earth-616)|S.H.I.E.L.D.]] is nothing more than "bullies with deep pockets," and makes Jessica an offer to work for Hydra, promising the protection of the organization in exchange for information. Cohen gives Jessica "a really big brick" of cash as proof of her commitment to working with Jessica, but Jessica threatens to "shove it up [Cohen's] ass."{{r|The Pulse Vol 1 8}}
