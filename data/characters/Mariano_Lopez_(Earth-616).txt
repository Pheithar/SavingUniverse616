| History                 = 
[[Alfonso Lopez (Earth-616)|Alfonso Lopez]] was inadvertently killed by [[Kali Vries (Earth-616)|Kali Vries]] while being brought in by [[Superhuman Tactical Activities Response Squad (Earth-616)|S.T.A.R.S.]] After his brother's death, Mariano Lopez next took up the identity of Machete. He was then recruited into [[Batroc's Brigade (Earth-616)|Batroc's Brigade]] like his brothers before him.

Later, Machete left for [[London (England)|London]] with his teammate [[Maximillian Zaran (Earth-616)|Zaran]] as part of a [[Radically Advanced Ideas in Destruction (Earth-616)|R.A.I.D.]] terrorist plot. Machete and Zaran were tasked with assassinating the members of the Houses of Parliament, but they were stopped by an ad hoc team of superheroes led by [[Joseph Chapman (Earth-616)|Union Jack]].{{r|Union Jack Vol 2 1}}
