| HistoryText             = The Great Captains of Krakoa was established by the [[Quiet Council of Krakoa (Earth-616)|Quiet Council of Krakoa]] for the purpose of advancing the interests of the nation's military. Each Captain of Krakoa is in charge of a specific jurisdiction. The Captain Commander is chief among all Captains.{{r|House of X Vol 1 6}} This post was initially held by [[Scott Summers (Earth-616)|Cyclops]], but after reforming the [[X-Men (Earth-616)|X-Men]] he stepped down from this post and was succeeded by [[Lucas Bishop (Earth-1191)|Bishop]]. {{r|Inferno Vol 2 1}} 

The four Great Captains of Krakoa and their jurisdictions are:{{r|X-Men Vol 5 4}}
* '''[[Lucas Bishop (Earth-1191)|Bishop]]:''' Captain Commander, member of the [[Marauders (Pryde) (Earth-616)|Marauders]] and the [[Hellfire Club (Earth-616)|Hellfire Trading Company]], acts as security for the Hellfire Trading Company's activities.
* '''[[Scott Summers (Earth-616)|Cyclops]]:''' Former Captain Commander and leader of the [[X-Men (Earth-616)|X-Men]].
* '''[[Illyana Rasputin (Earth-616)|Magik]]:''' Member of the [[New Mutants (Earth-616)|New Mutants]], acts in the [[Akademos Habitat|Sextant]] being mostly in charge of squadrons formed by younger generations of mutants.
* '''[[Kwannon (Earth-616)|Psylocke]]:''' Personal bodyguard for members of the [[Quiet Council of Krakoa (Earth-616)|Quiet Council of Krakoa]]. Succeeded [[Tomi Shishido (Earth-616)|Gorgon]] after his death in [[Otherworld]] left him unable to fulfill his duties.
