| History                 = ===Becoming 8-Ball===
After [[Jeff Hagees (Earth-616)|Jeff Hagees]] was killed by the [[Dirk Garthwaite (Earth-616)|Wrecker]], his gear he used as 8-Ball was sold by the [[Roderick Kingsley (Earth-616)|Hobgoblin]] to an unnamed criminal, who later came to serve under him.

===Goblin War===
8-Ball was present during a battle between Hobgoblin's forces and the [[Goblin Underground (Earth-616)|Goblin Underground]]. He, along with every other Hobgoblin franchise, joined Team Goblin when [[Norman Osborn (Earth-616)|Green Goblin]] apparently killed Hobgoblin, really Kingsley's butler [[Hobgoblin (Claude) (Earth-616)|Claude]].{{r|Superior Spider-Man Vol 1 26}}

===Sinister Six===
8-Ball later appeared as a member of [[Fritz von Meyer (Earth-616)|Swarm]]'s incarnation of the [[Sinister Six (Earth-616)|Sinister Six]].{{r|Spider-Man and the X-Men Vol 1 4}}

===Returning to Hobgoblin===
After dealing with [[Roger Gocking (Earth-616)|Porcupine]], Hobgoblin regained 8-Ball's allegiance to him.{{r|Spider-Woman Vol 6 14}}
