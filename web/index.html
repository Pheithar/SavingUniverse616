About Universe 616

Universe 616 is one of many marvel universes. The number 616 refers to the index of the universe in the big Marvel "MULTIVERSE", you heard that right, the marvel lore trancends one universe. This is among other things how they solved having characters with multiple arcs. As an example we all know of the belowed spiderpig, a different version of Spiderman, that occurs in another multiverse, making it very plausible. Who knows maybe we are also in a Marvel universe, where superpowers are just not present?
